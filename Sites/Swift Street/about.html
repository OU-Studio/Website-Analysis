ABOUT US

Bridging a Capital Gap to Advance University Innovation

Swift Street Capital is a physician-led venture capital firm focused on investing in seed and Series A stage life sciences companies developing innovative human health products and services. The fund is leveraging a broad and collaborative network of premier research universities and entrepreneurs to source deal flow combined with the general partners’ deep domain experience, extraordinary vision, and execution discipline to choose fund investments, help build portfolio companies and guide them to high value exits for investors.

INVESTMENT THESIS

Global populations are rapidly aging, and people’s expectation of a long and healthy life are rising which is driving increases in spending on healthcare and opportunities for companies that can provide new solutions that improve clinical outcomes and reduce costs

Legacy human health companies have grown too large to effectively innovate and rely mostly on acquisitions to enter new markets and gain market share, increasing the value of VC-backed market entrants

Entrepreneurial physicians are uniquely positioned to recognize human health companies with highest growth and exit potential and invest early at attractive valuations

The cost of founding, building and exiting start-up technology companies is falling rapidly in the age of generative AI, reducing capital needs and increasing early investor returns

Research universities located in underdeveloped capital markets generate investible opportunities at attractive valuations
